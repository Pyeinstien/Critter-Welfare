Our Services

Our ultimate objective involves creating a volunteer network along with assimilation of medical facilities to eradicate rabies from street animals in Delhi(NCR) thus reducing a danger factor involving them and increasing their acceptance into society by mass scale vaccination of street animals especially dog, cat and monkeys.
